# Privacy Policy — TOD Timecode

**Last updated: April 2026**

TOD Timecode ("the app") is a free camera sync utility 
developed by John Steventon.

## Data Collection
This app collects no personal data whatsoever. It does not:
- Collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- Use analytics or tracking
- Require an account or login
- Access your camera, microphone, or any device sensors
- Use advertising

The app reads only your device's system clock to display 
the current time as a timecode. This data never leaves 
your device.
